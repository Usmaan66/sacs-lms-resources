### July Revolution (France, 1830):

* **Overthrow:** Bourbon kings (restored after 1815) overthrown by liberal revolutionaries.
* **New Government:** Constitutional monarchy installed with Louis Philippe as head.
* **Metternich's Quote:** "When France sneezes, the rest of Europe catches cold."
* **Impact:** Sparked an uprising in Brussels, leading to Belgium breaking away from the United Kingdom of the Netherlands.
