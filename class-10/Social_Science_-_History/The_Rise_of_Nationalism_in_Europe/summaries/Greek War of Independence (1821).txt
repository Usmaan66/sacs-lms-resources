### Greek War of Independence (1821):

* **Background:** Greece had been part of the Ottoman Empire since the 15th century.
* **Spark:** Growth of revolutionary nationalism in Europe.
* **Support:** Greeks in exile and West Europeans (sympathetic to ancient Greek culture) supported the cause. Poets and artists (like Lord Byron, who died fighting in **1824**) lauded Greece as the "cradle of European civilisation."
* **Outcome:** Treaty of Constantinople of **1832** recognized Greece as an independent nation.
